PRISONOP

ABOUT

Prison is a type of interactive gamemode inspired by various games with a modest graphical interface. The help page gives you all the support to start your adventure on the section. The economy of the section is based on amplifying the powers of the pickaxe, called enchantments, but also by acquiring the higher rank, obtaining it with the help of money. You have 5 different enchantments with a maximum limit of 1000 levels. Tokenator, Coinsever, Speed, Explosive and Fortune help you boost your ability.
